On March 17th, 2009, Little Rock Wind, LLC, held a landowner meeting at the Clinton Memorial Building in Clinton, Minnesota. Landowners in our project footprint and the surrounding area were invited to attend and learn more about Little Rock Wind and the advantages of our community-based approach to wind development. The meeting was a great success with over 50 area landowners and community members in attendance.
