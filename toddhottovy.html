Todd Hottovy

Todd Hottovy is an insurance agent and owner of Hottovy Insurance Agency in Morris, MN, where he was born and raised. Todd enjoys competitive sports and spending quality time with his wife, Beth, and their three children.

With an eye toward “green technology,” Todd is involved in both Little Rock Wind LLC and another wind energy project. Todd says he believes it is important for our generation to lay the groundwork for cleaner and more responsible energy that benefits both our local economy today as well as generations down the line.
